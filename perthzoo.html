Perth Zoo

A Unique Contact With Nature

Fun fact: Turtles have existed for over 215 million years!

Perth Zoo wants to establish itself as the number one choice for a family day out, and something which provides an authentic opportunity to experience wildlife in well designed exhibits as well as providing a conservational message.

Under the idea of providing a “unique contact with nature” the emphasis is placed on the individual experience of a day out at the zoo and their interaction with the environment and animals.

This project was a university project during the Curtin University masterclass with Block Branding.

"Everybody loves animal facts"

The goal of the rebrand was to provide an authentic experience to the younger visitors especially that allows them to feel involved and up close with the wildlife, and to leave with more knowledge and inspiration. It had to create this experience cohesively throughout the park through its theme. Each piece of marketing was used as a way to show off some of the wildlife, and to share some facts, because everybody loves animal facts.

A Unique Contact With Nature.

I placed an emphasis on displaying information in an enjoyable and fun way, in order to promote the educational side, simplifying for the younger audiences. Additionally, the design needed to work over signage, stationary and digital. Design elements were created to cover each of these mediums.

I also designed an accompanying app that allowed Zoo goers to get the most out of their visit by finding information about animals and the park on the go, as well as reminders for keeper talks.

Through use of a consistent illustration style and basic infographics that accompany signage, it helps to get basic messages at a glance while also providing a fun and exciting way to view it for children. The bright colour scheme and illustrations were used throughout the park.
