The Turf

Sharing stories for the next generation of football fanatics.

View live site chevron_right

Since 2013, The Turf has focused on sharing stories from the terraces in Melbourne to the beaches of Rio de Janeiro. Taking a fresh approach to the game, the aim was to produce a fresh perspective for Australia’s youth football movement.

This project was a phase 2 designed to evolve The Turf to reflect the growth of the last few years for the brand, we wanted to be able to include new categories such as sneakers, gaming and lifestyle that often cross over with the target market.

V3 was all about evolving the brand, focusing on our strengths and including new subjects.

The new navigation aimed to provide an easier way for our users to navigate. Weak points I identified were the ability to search for content, in particular some of the higher quality articles such as exclusive player interviews and galleries. It was important to showcase them in a prominent location as well as rethinking the menu structure.
