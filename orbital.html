Orbital Corp.

Innovative Industrial Technology.

ORBITAL invents and builds smart technology that delivers improved performance outcomes for our clients in the safety & productivity and unmanned aerial vehicle sectors. We needed to create a more accomplished and modern look for the Orbital brand website, also being cohesive with newly acquired companies, Remsafe and UAVE. The new site had heavy emphasis on reflecting their stance as a leader in industrial innovation and providing world class capabilities.

Innovation that performs.

The key goals in the update to Orbitals website were providing much more information about the company as well as creating a better structure to explain its technology and sell its message. We also took the previously dark and heavy colour scheme to a lighter, modern and clean aesthetic that could make the technology the hero across each sector.

In addition to Orbitals service, the site had to promote the business of UAVE and Remsafe. While all having their own branding, the site had to link these together and show cohesiveness between all three.

Links to all three services are prominently displayed on the new homepage in addition to ASX announcements, events and news. While initially designed as individual sites that were created so that visually there would be a link between them, it was later decided to include them as standalone pages on the primary site.

In order to link the branding of the three different companies, I designed a visual element to be used throughout. The wave graphic adds a modern touch and is used as a secondary design element for Orbital, Remsafe and UAVE so they can have a common thread, while keeping their individual branding. As well as this, I developed a series of icons to further keep consistency between each.
