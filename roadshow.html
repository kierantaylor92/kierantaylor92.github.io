Village Roadshow

Website redesign concept

A project taken on while at Isobar, Roadshow wanted a full redesign of their website and this concept was the initial stage of those designs. Provided with the direction of moving to a dark theme, we looked at how to use the high quality imagery to promote their exciting titles and how to refine all the information currently shown on the site.

The goal

As the focus was mainly a visual concept only the homepage was presented. This was intended to lead into blog pages and product pages so it was important to create modules and styles that could be taken into those pages.

With access to some really inspirational shots from big name movies, these became the base of the design, allowing us to create separation from the black background and each of the titles.


The cutout treatment of hero sections enchanced the excitement of seeing the stars and would continue throughout into the product pages and potentially the 'Universe' sections, which would collect series within specific movie universes.

There was some double up when listing a movie name and it being included in the image. By using headings alongside the theatrical poster for hero titles and leaving components such as carousels with headings within the poster we could avoid that and reduce some of the clutter on the page.

Another issue was some confusion with the tags used alongside titles, being clear what was in cinema or on dvd, and whether the user would be clicking through to buy tickets externally or be provided with options to purchase a copy, as well as presenting the information such as genre and runtime, using clear language within the CTA's and focus on the heairarchy of the pills and secondary information allowed this to be presented much clearer.
